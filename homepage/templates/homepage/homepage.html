Dr. Sharanjit Kaur

A Ph.D from University of Delhi in the area of 'Mining Data Streams',her areas of interest in research are 'databses' and 'Data Mining'.
She is currently supervising M.Sc research in the area of 'Cloud Computing' at Department of Computer Science,University of Delhi.
She has organized multiple workshops using free and open source software(FOSS) to spread awareness among students in the futuristic avenues in FOSS paradigm.
She has also supervised students under 'Elite' Programme in 2012.She is also Supervising MSc(Computer Science) research project
as co-supervisor at Department of Computer Science,University of Delhi,since july 2012
